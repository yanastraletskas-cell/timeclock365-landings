Verified attendance — not self-reported hours

When you have 50, 200, or 500 employees, you cannot rely on everyone remembering to start and stop a timer. TimeClock 365 removes the variable.

🚪

Entry Is the Clock-In

Attendance is recorded the moment an employee enters via fingerprint, card, or mobile. There is no timer to start and no timesheet to fill out — the door does the work.

📍

GPS for Field Teams

Track employees who work outside the office by GPS check-in. Same dashboard, same reports — office and field staff managed in one place without switching tools.

👥

Team Visibility in Real Time

See who is in the building right now, who is late, and who has not checked in. Get alerts when attendance patterns fall outside your defined rules.

Payroll-Ready Output
